About Me

Jon Ferguson is an aspiring web developer and all around good human being from Austin Texas. Jon has worked for Apple Inc. for the past six years in a variety of technical and non-technical roles, most recently as an App Review Senior Advisor. It was his responsibility to handle escalated cases from App Review Advisors, and to ensure that Apple's relationships with their software developers were handled appropriately.

Jon is seeking a job as a junior developer on the strength of this portfolio. He learned to code at the Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin.
